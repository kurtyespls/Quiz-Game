How to Play?

Welcome to the Quiz Game!

Follow these steps to play:

  1. Enter your name in the Player Name field.
  2. Select a category from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click on the Start Game button to begin.
  4. Answer each question by clicking on the correct choice.
  5. Click Next to move to the next question.
  6. Finish all questions in the category to view your score.
  7. Click on Leaderboard to view the top scores.
  8. Have fun and enjoy the game!
